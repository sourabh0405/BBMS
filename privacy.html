Privacy Policy

Collection and Use of Your Information

The RedCross Donor protects your privacy by not collecting or sharing any personal information like names, passwords, or login details. Location Services: The RedCross donor app does access your location to find nearby blood banks. However, users can turn off location services if they prefer not to share it. The app will inform you if location services are not available when the app is started. Website: The eRaktKosh website might require some personal information in specific situations. Users will always be informed about why the information is needed and adequate security measures will be taken to protect their personal information.

Security of Your Information

CDAC respects and protects the privacy of the individuals who use RedCross donor Website/Mobile App services and is committed to helping protect the security of your information. We collect some basic information to understand how people interact with the Website/app. This includes things like your internet connection details (IP address), browser type, operating system, date and time of use, and the pages visited. We make no attempt to link these addresses with the identity of individuals visiting our site unless an attempt to damage the site has been detected. The application is, however, not capturing any individually identifiable information about you. Neither is the information shared with any third party.
